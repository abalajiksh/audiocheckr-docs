# Spectral Analysis

## Codec Cutoffs

| Codec | Bitrate | Cutoff |
|-------|---------|--------|
| MP3 | 128 kbps | 16 kHz |
| MP3 | 320 kbps | 20 kHz |

## Rolloff Steepness

| Steepness | Interpretation |
|-----------|---------------|
| < 20 dB/oct | Natural |
| > 60 dB/oct | Brick-wall |
